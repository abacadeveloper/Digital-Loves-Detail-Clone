Hi! i´m Mollie

… And I’m here to tell you that creating a curated home that looks designer doesn’t have to be “out of the budget”! In fact the room I designed that went viral and launched my instagram growth cost only $500 total!! I love combining my passions of interiors & bargain hunting to help you design a home that’s both beautiful & budget-friendly. My philosophy involves blending a few “reasonable splurges” with amazing bargain finds to achieve a high end look you’ll love!


Ever since I was a young girl I’ve been a bargain hunter, just like my Dad! He taught me that price isn’t necessarily and indicator of value & quality and that you can find great products at many different stores, which is why I like to scour the internet to bring you finds from a multitude of sources!


I was just 12 years old when I decided I wanted to be an interior designer! My mom let me paint murals on the walls of my room & bathroom as well as selecting the affordable furniture and decor for my room. I’m so glad my parents let me express my creativity at a young age and develop skills that I still use in my life today!


I’m a big believer that the spaces we live in matter more than we know! Our homes are one of the best places we can possibly invest because they’re where we spend most of our time. They’re the spaces we get to relax in, spend time with our families in, find peace & solace from an ever-changing world in. For me living in a home that is a reflection of me and my family brings me joy & comfort. This is why I want to help you create a space that feels inspired and intentional, too. These kinds of spaces can truly help us thrive in life.


My own home (which I coined #EuropeanOrganicModern and sums up my unique design aesthetic) is the 3rd home my husband Ben and I have designed/built together alongside my contractor brother-in-law, Spencer of Clearstone Builders. Ben and I have 4 busy & energetic children who keep life exciting as we take on home projects & our newest endeavor a YouTube channel to share our tips on getting the best bang for your buck when building a home, styling tips & product reviews! If you’ve followed me on instagram for long, you’ve probably seen I’m always on the move & always sharing the best deals of the day to get your home looking incredible for less. I’ve also got my finds linked here on my site under the “shop” tab! Now let’s get to designing home, one deal at a time!
